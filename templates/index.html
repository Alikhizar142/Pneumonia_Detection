About Pneumonia Detector

The Pneumonia Detector using X-ray images is an AI-powered system designed to assist healthcare professionals in the rapid and accurate diagnosis of pneumonia from chest X-ray scans. Leveraging deep learning algorithms and image recognition technology, it automatically analyzes X-ray images, identifies regions indicative of pneumonia, and provides a probability score for the presence of the condition. This innovative tool aims to improve diagnostic efficiency and patient outcomes by offering timely insights and decision support to healthcare providers.
