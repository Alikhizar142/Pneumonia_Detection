Project Description

Pneumonia Detection Project aims to develop a comprehensive solution for the early and accurate diagnosis of pneumonia using advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques. By leveraging the expertise of a multidisciplinary team and exploring the use of VGGNet and ResNet architectures, the project will deliver a user-friendly web-based platform that can assist healthcare professionals in improving patient outcomes through timely and reliable pneumonia detection.
